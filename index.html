Founder & CEO

Arun is the founder and CEO of EmpowerYouAcademy. He drives the academy's vision, strategy and programs. While pursuing corporate journey, Arun founded Early Leadership Academy in 2005 to teach leadership skills in children as young as middle and elementry school with a focus on learning-by-doing. He organized several hands-on workshops and summer camps. His lifelong passion for leadership, innovation, enterprenuership led him to launch EmpowerYouAcademy.

Technologist & CTO

Abhay is the technologist and CTO. He manages the technical strategy, and is dedicated to building a web presence to keep academy at the forefront of the technology trends. Abhay became an entrepreneur early on by building a software product, TimeM, while he was in high school. He is president of TinoInvestors club at Cupertino High School and pursuing aspirations in coding and building software products.
